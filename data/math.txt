Addition means putting numbers together.
Example: 2 + 3 = 5.

Subtraction means taking away.
Example: 5 - 2 = 3.

Multiplication means repeated addition.
Example: 3 × 2 means 3 + 3.

Word problems often ask:
- How many in total?
- How many left?
- How many more?

Solve slowly, one step at a time.
